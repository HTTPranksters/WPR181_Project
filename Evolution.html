Evolution of the Internet

Early Networking:
ARPANET and initial data-sharing experiments. The main goal was to connect institutions and universities.
TCP/IP Standardization:
Establishment of TCP/IP as the core protocol.
Commercialization:
Emergence of commercial ISPs and the dot-com boom.
Web 2.0:
User-generated content, social media, and interactive web applications.
Mobile Internet:
Internet access via mobile devices and the rise of apps.
Internet of Things (IoT):
Integration of everyday objects into the Internet.
